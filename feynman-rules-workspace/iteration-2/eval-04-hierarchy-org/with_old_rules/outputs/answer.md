Typical software engineering org hierarchy (4 levels):

```
VP of Engineering
├── Director of Engineering (Platform)
│   ├── Engineering Manager — Platform team
│   │   ├── Staff SWE
│   │   ├── Senior SWE × 2
│   │   └── SWE × 3
│   └── Engineering Manager — Infra team
│       ├── Senior SWE × 2
│       └── SWE × 2
└── Director of Engineering (Product)
    ├── Engineering Manager — Frontend
    │   ├── Senior SWE
    │   └── SWE × 2
    └── Engineering Manager — Backend
        ├── Senior SWE
        └── SWE × 3
```

**Level responsibilities:**
- **VP** — engineering strategy, headcount, cross-functional alignment
- **Directors** — team roadmaps, hiring plans, cross-team dependencies
- **Managers** — sprint execution, performance reviews, technical decisions escalation
- **ICs (SWEs)** — feature delivery, code review, on-call rotation
