<objective>
Measure all 7 arms on the 50-prompt corpus in two waves; pass sanity gate between waves; produce REPORT.md with explicit winner or refuted conclusion.

Wave 1 (baselines): v0.2.x (reused from smoke-1) + v0.3.x (fresh) + v0.3.x+ladder (fresh)
Wave 2 (candidates): v05-A, v05-B, v05-C, v05-ABC (4 parallel subagents)

Sanity gate: v0.3.x / v0.2.x delta on 50p must be 21–41% (matching Phase 11's +31% ±10pp).
Winner threshold: ≥−20% verbosity vs v0.3.x AND ≥95% lint compliance.
</objective>

<design_decisions>
v0.2.x reuse: results-v02-smoke-1.json was already a valid 50-prompt run. Renamed to results-v02-50p.json with arm="v0.2.x". This avoids one redundant subagent run. Documented in REPORT.md.

Sanity gate range: Phase 11 measured +31.2% gap (v0.3.x avg 267 vs v0.2.x avg 204 chars on 15p corpus). ±10pp gives acceptable range [21%, 41%]. If 50p baseline lands outside this range, corpus is biased and Phase 14 rebalance is triggered before proceeding.

Parallelism cap: Wave 2 runs 4 subagents simultaneously. Wave 1 runs 2. Total: never more than 4 concurrent to avoid ECONNRESET.
</design_decisions>
