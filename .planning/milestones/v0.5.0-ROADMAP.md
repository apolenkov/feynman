# Roadmap Archive: feynman v0.5.0 — Verbosity Economy

**Milestone:** v0.5.0 — Verbosity Economy
**Shipped:** 2026-05-11
**Phases:** 14–18
**npm:** `@albinocrabs/feynman@0.5.0`

## Overview

Milestone goal: reduce verbosity of model responses produced under feynman rules
by ≥20%, without sacrificing ASCII diagram compliance. Method: empirical
evaluation — 7-arm measurement on a 50-prompt corpus, winner applied to
production rules.

Outcome: winner v0.5.x-ABC applied, −54.7% verbosity vs v0.4.x baseline,
364/364 tests green.
